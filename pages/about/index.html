About

Hello, I'm Rufus Fraanje (baptized as Petrus Rufus Fraanje, after my grandfather Pieter Fraanje). I live in Delft, the Netherlands, where I work as robotics and control lecturer and researcher at The Hague University of Applied Sciences (THUAS). Besides my work, I enjoy walking in nature, alone and with friends, playing the trumpet (Martin Committee) and taking part in the local church community of the Immanuelkerk.

You can contact me through private email, work email (please remove the anti-spam characters) or Linked-In. You may find most of my publications at Google Scholar. Some of my programming projects are available from Github.
